ADIYOGI

Adiyogi, a manifestation of the divine in Hinduism, symbolizes the primal yogi and source of yogic sciences. Revered as a spiritual guide, Adiyogi's teachings encompass various aspects of life, emphasizing self-realization and the path to transcendence.

Statue of euality

The Statue of Equality, located in Hyderabad, India, is a monumental representation of the revered Indian philosopher and social reformer, Sri Ramanuja Acharya. Standing at 216 feet, it aims to promote equality, inclusivity, and the teachings of Sri Ramanuja, fostering a message of unity and social harmony..

Murdeshwar

The Murdeshwar Temple in Karnataka, India, features a towering Lord Shiva statue standing at 123 feet, making it one of the tallest statues in the country. This iconic sculpture is set against the Arabian Sea, creating a breathtaking visual spectacle and attracting pilgrims and tourists alike..
